# Sovereign Core: Strategic Vision & Theoretical Alignment
**Framework for Gemini Notebook Integration**

## 1. Liberation Psychology Digital Implementation
The Sovereign Core Project moves beyond the "Recovery Model" (which implies a return to a prior state) to the "Liberation Model" (which implies moving beyond the original systemic structures).
*   **Digital Aspect:** The app does not "fix" the user; it provides the user with tools to "fix" the system.

## 2. Critical Race Theory (CRT) in UI/UX
*   **Counter-Storytelling:** The Education Portal is a digital venue for counter-storytelling, allowing youth of color to redefine their experiences outside of "at-risk" statistics.
*   **Intersectionality:** The System Map identifies how power intersections (Housing + Race + Age) create the barriers they experience.

## 3. Diffusion of Power
*   The project explicitly rejects "Adultism" by placing Lived Experience Consultants at the head of content creation (The Sovereign Creators Fellowship).
*   Technical execution ensures that even the AI models are guided by youth-defined parameters, not institutional clinical definitions.

## 4. Desirable Effects & Sovereign Outcomes
*   **Internal Outcome:** Authentic identity perception regardless of housing.
*   **Systemic Outcome:** Legislative participation and the dismantlement of paternalistic housing policy.
